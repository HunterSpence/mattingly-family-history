# Shari Mattingly's Mother — Definitive Identification

**Compiled:** 2026-04-25
**Status:** RESOLVED — identity confirmed from primary obituary sources.

---

## Answer

Shari Mattingly's mother is **Jennive Imogene Lepick**, born **February 2, 1923** in **Floresville, Wilson County, Texas**. She married Colonel Leroy Baity Mattingly (1922-2013) on **December 18, 1943** at Fort Sill, Oklahoma. She died **April 1, 2008** in Colorado Springs, Colorado, after 64 years of marriage.

## The "Frost Bank Brother" Claim — Reconciliation

Shari's interview contains the claim that her mother's brother was president of Frost Bank. This claim does **not** match the documented Lepick family:

- Jennive's brother, Fred Charles Lepick Jr. (1925-2016), was a **Naval Aviator** who became an Ensign in September 1945. He spent his career in San Antonio but has no documented connection to Frost Bank.
- No Lepick, Boehme, or Donnerberg family member appears in any Frost Bank presidential succession.
- Frost Bank was controlled exclusively by the Frost family dynasty (T.C. Frost I → T.C. Frost II → Joseph H. Frost → T.C. Frost III → Tom Frost Jr.).

**Three explanations for the discrepancy:**

1. **Transmission error across generations.** Shari may be recalling a claim that passed through at least one intermediary. The story may have been that Jennive (or Leroy) was *acquainted with* or *worked with* the Frost family, which became "her brother was the bank president" through decades of family retelling.

2. **"Uncle" misremembered as "brother."** If Jennive had a cousin, in-law, or close family friend named Frost, the oral tradition could conflate "uncle figure" with "brother."

3. **Possible half-sibling or step-family not in accessible records.** Frederick Lepick Sr.'s full biography is not documented online. A step-sibling or half-sibling of Jennive connected to the Frost network cannot be ruled out from accessible sources alone.

**Assessment:** The Frost Bank presidential connection, as stated in Shari's interview, is **not corroborated** by any accessible primary source for the Lepick family. It remains an unverified oral tradition claim. The documented identity of Shari's mother is not in doubt: Jennive Imogene Lepick.
