## Frost Bank — Shari's Maternal Line

### Thomas Claiborne Frost (31 Dec 1833-21 Nov 1903) — Frost Bank Founder

- Born: Belle Font, Jackson County, Alabama
- Parents: Thomas C. and Crissy (Price) Frost
- Wife 1: Betty Eastland Roberts (m. 30 Nov 1858, Belton TX; d. before 1878) — several children; only one daughter survived to adulthood
- Wife 2: Josephine Houston (m. 31 Dec 1878) — four children total
- **Confirmed sons:** Thomas C. Frost Jr. (bank president to 1926) and Joseph Hardin Frost (bank president from 1926)
- *Source: TSHA Handbook of Texas; Wikipedia Frost Bank*

### The Grandmother Identification Problem

**Generational math:** If Shari's mother was born 1923, and her "maternal uncle was president of Frost Bank," the most likely scenario is:
- Shari's maternal grandmother was a sibling of Joseph Hardin Frost
- Joseph Hardin Frost (president 1926) = Shari's maternal great-uncle, not uncle
- Or: "uncle" is used loosely and Joseph Hardin IS the uncle directly — meaning Shari's grandmother was T.C. Frost's daughter

**Either way, T.C. Frost's children (from wife 2, Josephine Houston, m.1878) are the targeted generation.** The TSHA names T.C. Jr. and Joseph H. as sons but does not enumerate daughters. The Chabot 1937 book ("With the Makers of San Antonio," Yanaguana Society Publications 4, San Antonio) is the key source; it is cited in the TSHA bibliography and likely names all T.C. Frost's children.

**Highest-priority action: Ask Shari her mother's maiden name.** If the answer is "Frost," then trace from T.C. Frost's daughters via the Chabot 1937 book.
