## Confirmed public record findings

### Dr. Dale William Spence Sr.

- Rice Athletics states that Dr. Dale Spence won the 1952 Texas state title in the 880 while competing for **Beaumont High School**, ran for Rice from **1952-1956**, later served in the Marine Corps, earned graduate degrees at North Texas and LSU, completed a Baylor postdoc, and was on the Rice faculty from **1963 to 2003**.
  - Confidence: HIGH
  - Source: https://riceowls.com/news/2019/10/11/mens-track-field-pr-club-to-honor-spence-stadel-and-1995-championship-team

- A Beaumont High School yearbook page for 1952 names **Dale Spence** among the track standouts and reports that he won a state championship that season.
  - Confidence: HIGH
  - Source: https://www.e-yearbook.com/yearbooks/Beaumont_High_School_Pine_Burr_Yearbook/1952/Page_234.html

- A 2020 Rice History Corner comment posted under the name **Dale Spence, Colonel, USMCR (Ret)** says his Rice undergraduate years were **1952-1956** and ties him directly to the Marine Corps commissioning cohort from that class period.
  - Confidence: HIGH
  - Source: https://ricehistorycorner.com/2020/05/07/semper-fidelis-date-unknown/

- LSU's dissertation repository gives the full name **Dale William Spence** and shows that he received an **EdD in 1966**.
  - Confidence: HIGH
  - Source: https://repository.lsu.edu/gradschool_disstheses/1171/

- PubMed records a 1990 paper by **D.W. Spence** with affiliation to the **Department of Human Performance and Health Sciences, Rice University, Houston, TX 77251-1892**.
  - Confidence: HIGH
  - Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/2375667/

- Rice News in 2019 refers to **Dale Spence, professor emeritus of kinesiology**.
  - Confidence: HIGH
  - Source: https://news2.rice.edu/2019/11/11/honoring-our-veterans/
