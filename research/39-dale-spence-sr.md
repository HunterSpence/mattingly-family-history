## Critical Correction: Dale Sr. Is American-Born

**Dale William Spence Sr. is NOT himself an English immigrant.**

Family tradition holds that he "came from England on a boat." The public record refutes a direct-generation reading of that tradition. Dale Sr. competed as a Beaumont, Texas high school student in 1952, enrolled at Rice University that same year, and spent his entire adult life in Texas. He is American-born.

**The "England" connection attaches to his FATHER** — Hunter's paternal great-grandfather, an English-born man (name currently unverified) who immigrated to Beaumont, Texas approximately 1920–1935, drawn by the oil-industry boom following Spindletop (1901). He married Dovie, an American-born Southern woman, and their son Dale William Spence Sr. was the first fully American generation of this Spence line.

Confidence: CONFIRMED for Dale Sr.'s American birth and Texas upbringing; PROBABLE for the father-as-immigrant interpretation.
