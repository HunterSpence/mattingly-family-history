**Source:** "Fayette County, Her History and Her People" (1902), p.229 (IIIF sequence page 251). Library of Congress item `02023423`.

**License:** Public domain (published 1902).

**Notes:** The biography page (LOC sequence 251) begins "HANS TEICHMUELLER.
